How to Play Lucky Duck

The goal of the game is to feed all the ducks on the screen by bringing them bread. You can move the bread by clicking on the duck, dragging the bread to the duck, or moving the bread with the arrow keys and throwing it with the space bar.

Happy Feeding!

A Note on Feeding Ducks

Feeding ducks or other wild birds bread is not good for their health. Next time you wish to go feed the ducks at your local pond, please try using produce (lettuce is a good, cheap option) or whole grains/seeds.
